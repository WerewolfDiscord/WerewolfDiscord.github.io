Hey there, @everyone! Welcome to werewolves! Some of you may know this game, some may know a similar (forum) game called Mafia, but in this channel, you can read how the game exactly works.

The game is simple. You need to stay alive until the game ends. If the game is over and you're alive, you win. If you're dead before the game ends, you lose.
For more details, please read the other posts over @here.
These are the most important roles, @everyone

INNOCENTS

All roles in this category share one goal; kill every member that is part of the Werewolf team.

Innocents
This is the basic role of the game. Find out who the werewolves are, and vote for them to kill them off!

Bakers
Bakers know who all the other bakers are. They are all innocent.

The runner
The runner will escape death if the werewolves attempt to eat him. This only works once.

Fortune teller
Each night, the fortune teller can see the role of one player.

Huntress
When the huntress dies, she can choose to kill another player.

Cursed Civilian
If this player gets eaten by the werewolves, he will turn into a werewolf.

WEREWOLVES

Werewolf
You are a werewolf! It is your job to kill everyone who is not a werewolf.

White Werewolf
This werewolf wins if they are the only player alive. They will act like a normal werewolf, but will also try to eliminate all werewolves.

Warlock
The warlock is a corrupted fortune teller. The role works the same as the fortune teller, but the warlock wins when the werewolves win.

These are the simple roles, @everyone. There are many more, but you can find them further down.
What happens during daytime.
Every day, the town points out to a suspect who they think might be a werewolf. At the end of the day, the person that has the most votes on him on the poll in #s1_story_time, will get killed in front of the whole town.
Remember: if you're about to get killed, you may bluff, lie, pretend and make up the weirdest stories if that makes you stay alive.

What happens during nighttime.
While the town is sleeping, several Secret Channels become active. Here, people will do special actions during the night that will have effect on the progress of the game during the day.
Usually, the werewolves choose a prey and eat it.

`Other rules:
If you die, you are out of the game and you have lost. There is no way to undo your death, and you will lose permission to talk on this server.
Though this is hard to control, please do not talk about the game outside of this server. Since I can't keep track of it, please do it as a gentlemen's agreement. When you're saying something to someone else, everybody else is supposed to hear it. Sending DMs could ruin the game.
You are not allowed to vote for yourself during the kill-vote.

When is it nighttime, when is it daytime?
The days are counted as real-life days, which means that the days are from 09:00 GMT until 20:00` GMT.

As @everyone may have noticed, we have a new bot around! @everyone, please welcome @Werewolf Bot!
The Werewolf Bot is supposed to make the game easier to manage, and will make sure that administrating will cost way less time! Most importantly, you will no longer have to wait 5 whole minutes for your own secret channel! In contrary, you will gain them almost instantly, now that the bot will do the work for you!

Here you can look up the most imporant roles.
Commands @everyone can use outside of sessions:
~a request :emoji: - Asks the @Game Masters to sign up with an emoji as your avatar.

Commands @everyone can use during the game:
~c create <name> <user1> <user2> <user3> ... - Creates a conspiracy channel

If you created a conspiracy channel, you can also use these commands:
~c add <user> - Adds a user to the conspiracy channel
~c adda <emoji>- Adds the user to the conspiracy channel who has that emoji
~c remove <user> - Removes a user from the conspiracy channel
~c removea <emoji> - Removes the user from the conspiracy channel who has that emoji

Don't worry, you can request conspiracy channels anywhere, even inside other conspiracy channels. Oh, and don't worry about people reading the command; if you type it correctly, your message will be deleted immediately, and nobody will know you ever created that conspiracy channel.
Make sure that the name of the channel is ONE word; if you use multiple words, add _ in-between, else Discord won't accept it. And that's the most important; DON'T FORGET TO ADD A NAME TO YOUR CONSPIRACY CHANNEL if you want the bot to accept it.

How to sign up:


1. Pick your favourite emoji
Choose any emoji that is from the standard set. The bot hasn't gone Nitro yet, so we're limited to the vanilla emojis.

2. Go to the channel #signup.
Click on it; #signup, or find the channel yourself. Not too difficult. 🤗

3. Type your request to join for the bot to read!
Type ~a request <emoji>. For example, I love the 😏 emoji, so in my case, I should type ~a request 😏. Did your message get deleted immediately? Good, because that means the bot has sent a request to the @Game Masters! Is it still there? Then either the bot is offline (check @Werewolf Bot), or you misspelled something. Then check again.

The @Game Masters would like to help you anytime during your process of signing up.
